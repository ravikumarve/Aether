The Execution Pipeline

Strict quality gates and conditional mediation loops govern every cycle, guaranteeing an uncompromised continuous loop of survival logistics.

Phase 1: System Initialization

Loads core environment variables, validates MQTT connections, and boots the SimPy engine. Quality gate verifies all agents are registered and active.

Phase 2: Solara Power Audit

Scans telemetry for current battery levels and solar radiation. Generates a 24-hour forecast with dynamic confidence scoring (>92% required during daytime).

Phase 3: Veridian Biological Audit

Calculates precise water, light, and power draw necessary to maintain O2 > 19.5%. Verifies the request against Solara's projected power ceiling.

Phase 4: Hal-90 Mediation

Conditionally triggered if biological requests exceed power budgets. Hal-90 enforces the Goldilocks Zone constraints (18-26°C), arbitrating a final resource allocation.

Continuous Anomaly Loop

Listens via MQTT for sudden pressure drops, dust storms, or thermal spikes. Instantly classifies the anomaly and routes an emergency response sequence to the appropriate agent.
